RACHAEL ANDREW

About Rachael

Rachael Andrew is a highly respected figure in the web development community, known for her expertise in CSS, HTML, and web standards.

She is a co-founder of Perch CMS, a prolific writer, speaker, and mentor in the tech industry. Her work has had a significant impact on the way web developers design and build websites, and she continues to be a leading voice in the industry.

Notable Achievements

Author of CSS: The Definitive Guide: Rachael Andrew is the author of CSS: The Definitive Guide, a comprehensive guide to CSS layout and design.

Co-founder of Perch CMS: Rachael Andrew is the co-founder of Perch CMS, a popular content management system for web developers.

Founder of the CSS Working Group: Rachael Andrew is a founding member of the CSS Working Group, a group of web developers who work to define and maintain the CSS standard.

Speaker and Mentor: Rachael Andrew is a frequent speaker at web development conferences and events, and she is known for her mentorship and advocacy for web standards.
